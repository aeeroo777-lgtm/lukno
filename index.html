Our Process

The Art of Craftsmanship

01

Fine Fabric Selection

We source only the finest cotton, georgette and chiffon — breathable, ethereal, and worthy of art.

02

Pattern Design

Master artisans sketch intricate motifs — florals, paisleys, and geometric jalis — inspired by Mughal heritage.

03

Hand Embroidery

Skilled kaarigar embroider each piece by hand — a single kurti may take 3 to 7 days of delicate work.

04

Quality Assurance

Every piece passes a 12-point quality check before it reaches you — pristine, perfect, and worthy of your wardrobe.

Our Heritage

Born from the
City of Nawabs

Lucknow Chikan was born in the ancient lanes of the Nawabs — where Chikankari embroidery has been a living tradition for over three centuries. We are not just a brand; we are custodians of an art form that once adorned royalty.

Every thread we use carries the weight of history. Every pattern we embroider tells a story of patience, love, and mastery passed down through generations of artisan families.
